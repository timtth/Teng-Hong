### 👋 Hi, I'm Teng Hong

I'm a Research Assistant at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), where I apply Machine Learning and Data Science to solve real-world challenges in Materials Science. My research focuses on developing predictive models using additive manufacturing data to better understand and predict metal fatigue.

I'm passionate about building end-to-end machine learning solutions, transforming complex data into meaningful insights, and applying AI to solve impactful real-world problems. I enjoy continuously learning new technologies and expanding my skills in machine learning, data science, and software development.

**🔬 Current Interests**

- Machine Learning & Artificial Intelligence
- Data Science & Predictive Analytics
- Gradient Boosting & Deep Learning
- Feature Engineering & Model Optimization
- MLOps & End-to-End ML Pipelines
- Scientific Computing & Engineering AI

**🚀 Career Goal**

My long-term goal is to become a Data Scientist or Machine Learning Engineer, building intelligent systems that transform complex data into meaningful insights and real-world impact. I'm always excited to collaborate on open-source projects, research, and innovative applications of AI.
